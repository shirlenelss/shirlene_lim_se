## Denial of Responsibility
- "had to" - vague impersonal force
- "because I am " - condition, diagnosis, person , psychological history - personalized with it
- "hit my child because he xx xx " - actions of other
- "lied because was asked to" - authority
- "friends/family did the same" - group pressure
- "because of my role, social role, age role" - blame on role
- overcome my urge to eat the candy - impulses
